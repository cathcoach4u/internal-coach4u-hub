Your steps

What you need to do

1
Check your email from Gallup
You'll receive an invitation email from Gallup with a personalised link to your assessment. It comes from an automated Gallup address.
⚠️ Check your spam or junk folder if you don't see it within a few hours. The link is unique to you and expires — don't forward it to anyone else.
2
Complete the assessment
Set aside around 30–45 minutes somewhere quiet. The assessment presents a series of paired statements and asks which resonates most with you.
Tips for getting the most accurate result
  • Go with your first instinct — don't overthink it
  • There are no right or wrong answers
  • Answer based on how you actually are, not how you think you should be
  • Each response has a 20-second timer — trust your gut
  • Complete it in one sitting if you can
  • Find a quiet place free from interruptions
😌 This is one of the most useful things you can do before our session — the more honest your answers, the richer our conversation.
3
Let Cath know you're done
Once you've completed the assessment, send Cath a quick message. You don't need to do anything with the results yourself — Cath will access and prepare everything from her end.
