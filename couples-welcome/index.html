The process

What to expect


1
Free connection call
30 minutes · No charge
A complimentary consultation to understand what is happening, answer your questions, and explain how the process works. This is an opportunity to check fit before committing.
2
Couples intake session
2 hours · $500 + GST
A comprehensive intake session where formal documentation begins. The focus is on building safety, understanding relationship patterns, and clarifying goals and priorities for the work ahead.
3
Ongoing sessions
1.5 hours · $375 + GST · Usually fortnightly
Regular sessions focused on practical strategies, communication patterns, emotional safety, and strengthening the relationship. Approaches include EFT, Gottman, Imago, Narrative Therapy, and strengths-based work.
All sessions are conducted online via Microsoft Teams. Phone sessions are available if required.

Optional add-on

Strengths-based assessment

Many couples choose to complete a Gallup CliftonStrengths assessment as part of their work together. Understanding each partner's natural strengths provides a concrete, positive foundation for the coaching process — shifting focus toward what each person brings, rather than only what is broken.

Cath is trained in strengths-based couples work and can integrate individual and paired strengths profiles into sessions. The Top 5 or Full 34 themes are available. This is discussed during or after the intake session.
