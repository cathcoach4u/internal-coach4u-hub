Step 2 of 5

What is happening, just the facts

Describe what is going on, like a neutral person would see or hear it.

No interpretation yet. What happened, when, what was said or done.

Step 3 of 5

Why this matters

Name the impact, then what you want instead.

What is the cost to you, the relationship, the outcome, and what would good look like.

Step 4 of 5

What is underneath

What are you feeling right now, what do you need, what value matters here, what are you afraid of.

Often the real issue is about something the situation is threatening or denying you.

Step 5 of 5

The real issue, in one sentence

Specific, usable, something you can act on.

Saying it cleanly is half the work.

If this is mostly about you
The real issue is that I need [need or value] and I am not getting it because [specific barrier], so I will [small change I can control].
If this involves others
The real issue is the pattern of [pattern], the impact is [impact], the need underneath is [need], the next step is [small step].
