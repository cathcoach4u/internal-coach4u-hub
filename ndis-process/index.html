What happens next

1
Complete the intake form
The intake form gives the team the background information needed to prepare for the first session. It takes around 10–15 minutes and can be completed by the participant themselves or by a carer or guardian on their behalf.
📄 The form includes consent to our privacy and cancellation policies, so please read through when completing it.

Once the team receives the completed intake form, we'll send through a calendar invite to confirm the first session time.
