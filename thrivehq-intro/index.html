What is it

ThriveHQ Group Coaching

ThriveHQ is a small group coaching membership designed for adults with ADHD. It combines weekly group sessions, peer community, and practical tools to help you build structure, focus, and momentum in your daily life.

It's not a course or a one-off workshop — it's an ongoing membership with consistent support, accountability, and a community of people who genuinely get it.

What's included

Your membership includes

👥
Weekly group coaching sessions
Live sessions focused on planning, accountability, and working through what's getting in your way.
🧠
Executive functioning tools
Practical strategies for focus, planning, task initiation, and managing the day-to-day challenges of ADHD.
👥
FocusHQ body-doubling sessions
Structured co-working sessions — show up, get things done, and stay accountable alongside others.
🤝
Peer community
A small group of people who understand ADHD from the inside — not just observers, but participants working through the same things.
🌟
Strengths-based coaching approach
Sessions are built around what you're already good at — not just fixing what isn't working.
