In the meantime

A few things to reflect on

You don't need to prepare anything formal — but if you'd like to arrive ready for a richer conversation, consider these:

  • Which patterns in how you think or work have always felt completely natural to you?
  • Where do you find yourself energised by a task — and where does it feel like a drain?
  • When have you been at your best — at work, at home, or in relationships?
  • Is there anything you've always done instinctively that others seem to find difficult?
