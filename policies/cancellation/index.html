Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

How we manage appointment changes, fairly for everyone.

Your appointment is reserved exclusively for you. Because late changes leave limited time to offer your spot to someone else, we ask that cancellations and reschedules follow the timeframes below. This keeps our schedule dependable and respects every client's time.

Cancellation Timeframe

You can cancel or reschedule without any fee up to the cut-off times below:

Cut-off 9:00am on the business day before your appointment.
For a Monday appointment, notice must reach Coach4U by 9:00am on the Friday before.

Business days are Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays.

Late Cancellations & Non-Attendance

Cancellations made after 9:00am on the business day before your appointment, or failure to attend a scheduled session, result in:

The full session fee applies.

We do recognise that life can be challenging. If we are able to accommodate a time change within the same week, this can be discussed — however, this is not always guaranteed and will depend on availability.

Illness & Accident

In the event of sudden illness or accident, fees may be waived. These situations are considered with care and discretion — please reach out as early as you can so we can support you.

Why this matters

Late cancellations make it very difficult to offer the time to another client and reduce the availability of coaching and counselling for others. This policy lets us continue providing a dependable service and a fair schedule for everyone.

Questions?

If you're unsure about anything, or need to let us know about a change, please get in touch.
