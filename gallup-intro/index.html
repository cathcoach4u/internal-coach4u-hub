CliftonStrengths — What It Is & How It Works

Understanding the assessment, what you'll get, and how it fits into your coaching.

What is it

The CliftonStrengths Assessment

CliftonStrengths is a Gallup-developed assessment that identifies your natural patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving. It ranks your 34 strength themes in order — from your most dominant talents to those that are less natural to you.

It's not a personality test. It focuses on what you're already wired to do well, rather than what you're not.

How it works

What happens next

1
Let Cath know you'd like to proceed
Send Cath a message to confirm you'd like to go ahead with the CliftonStrengths Full 34 assessment.
💬 Message Cath on WhatsApp
2
Cath arranges your assessment
As an accredited Gallup Strengths Coach, Cath purchases the assessment on your behalf and sends it to your email address. You'll receive an invitation from Gallup directly.

The assessment takes around 30–45 minutes. You'll receive a separate guide with everything you need to know before you sit down to complete it.

3
Complete and notify Cath
Once you've completed the assessment, let Cath know. She'll access your results, prepare your personalised report, and bring it into your next session.
