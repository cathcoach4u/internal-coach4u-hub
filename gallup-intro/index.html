What is it

The CliftonStrengths Assessment

CliftonStrengths is a Gallup-developed assessment that identifies your natural patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving. It ranks all 34 strength themes in order — from your most dominant talents to those that take more energy.

It's not a personality test and there are no right or wrong answers. It focuses entirely on what you're already wired to do well — and that's exactly why Cath uses it as a coaching tool.
