Sessions & billing
👥 The client is the couple. Sessions are designed for both of you together — they require both partners to be present and cannot proceed with one person absent.
A few things to keep in mind about how sessions work:
  • The session begins when both partners are on the call
  • If either partner needs to leave, the session will close at that point — it cannot continue with one person absent. For example, if a session is scheduled for 90 minutes and one partner leaves after 60 minutes, the session ends at 60 minutes
  • The full scheduled session time is the basis for invoicing regardless of when either partner joins or leaves
  • Sessions may sometimes finish a little early or run slightly over depending on what's needed — the scheduled time remains the basis for billing either way
