Couples Counselling & Coaching — Process & Pricing

How sessions work, what to expect at each stage, and what it costs.

The process

What to expect


1
Free connection call
30 minutes · No charge
A complimentary consultation to understand what is happening, answer your questions, and explain how the process works. This is an opportunity to check fit before committing.
2
Couples intake session
2 hours
A comprehensive intake session where formal documentation begins. The focus is on building safety, understanding relationship patterns, and clarifying goals and priorities for the work ahead.
3
Ongoing sessions
1.5 hours
Regular sessions focused on practical strategies, communication patterns, emotional safety, and strengthening the relationship. Approaches include EFT, Gottman, Imago, Narrative Therapy, and strengths-based work.
All sessions are conducted online via Microsoft Teams.
